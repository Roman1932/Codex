# content-strategy

Use when planning content strategy, deciding what content to create, or choosing topics, clusters, and calendars.

## Main jobs

- define content goals
- map topics to funnel stages
- build topic clusters
- identify content gaps
- prioritize content ideas
- plan editorial calendar
- align content with SEO and product positioning

## Inputs

- product and audience
- market/language
- current content inventory
- target keywords or themes
- competitors
- business goal

## Output

- content pillars
- topic clusters
- article/page ideas
- priority list
- content calendar
- internal linking guidance
- measurement plan
