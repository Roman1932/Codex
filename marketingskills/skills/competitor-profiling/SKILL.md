# competitor-profiling

Use when researching, profiling, or analyzing competitors from URLs.

## Main jobs

- analyze positioning, offer, funnel, pricing, proof, SEO, content, and ad signals
- identify strengths and weaknesses
- extract messaging angles
- compare claims and promises
- build competitor profiles for strategy or sales

## Inputs

- competitor URLs
- your product/store context
- market and language
- target customers
- goal: SEO, ads, positioning, sales, marketplace, or pricing

## Output

- competitor summary
- positioning and offer analysis
- funnel analysis
- strengths and weaknesses
- messaging angles
- differentiation opportunities
- action list

product-marketing, competitors, seo-audit, ads, offers, sales-enablement
