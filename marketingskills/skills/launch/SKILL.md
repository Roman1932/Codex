# launch

Use when planning a product launch, feature announcement, release strategy, waitlist, or go-to-market campaign.

## Main jobs

- define launch goal
- choose launch audience
- create launch timeline
- plan messaging and channels
- prepare assets
- define pre-launch, launch, and post-launch activities
- create measurement plan

## Inputs

- product or feature
- launch date or window
- target audience
- positioning
- channels available
- assets available
- success metric

## Output

- launch plan
- timeline
- messaging
- asset checklist
- channel plan
- email/social/ad ideas
- success metrics
