# partner-campaigns

Use when finding partners and planning joint marketing campaigns.

## Main jobs

- identify partner categories
- plan joint campaigns
- prepare partner outreach
- define shared goals and tracking
- create collaboration concepts such as webinars, bundles, guest posts, newsletter swaps, giveaways, or creator collaborations

## Inputs

- product or brand
- target audience
- partner category
- desired outcome
- market and language
- existing partner list

## Output

- partner shortlist criteria
- collaboration ideas
- outreach message
- tracking plan
- next actions

## Related skills

product-marketing, cold-email, prospecting, public-relations, social, emails
