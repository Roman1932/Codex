# customer-research

Use when conducting, analyzing, or synthesizing customer research.

## Main jobs

- analyze customer interviews, reviews, surveys, tickets, and feedback
- extract pain points and buying triggers
- identify objections and desired outcomes
- capture customer language
- build jobs-to-be-done insights

## Inputs

- customer feedback source
- product context
- target segment
- research question
- market/language

## Output

- key themes
- verbatim customer language
- jobs-to-be-done
- pain points
- objections
- messaging implications
- product/marketing recommendations

product-marketing, copywriting, offers, cro, content-strategy
