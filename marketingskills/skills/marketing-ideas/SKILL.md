# marketing-ideas

Use when generating marketing ideas, growth strategies, campaign concepts, channel ideas, or creative approaches.

## Main jobs

- brainstorm growth ideas
- create channel ideas
- generate campaign angles
- suggest quick wins
- create experiments
- adapt ideas to audience and market

## Inputs

- product and audience
- current channels
- business goal
- constraints
- market/language
- budget/time capacity

## Output

- idea list
- prioritized opportunities
- low-effort quick wins
- experiment concepts
- channel recommendations
- next actions
