# community-marketing

Use when building and leveraging online communities to drive product growth and brand loyalty.

## Main jobs

- plan community strategy
- define community purpose and member profile
- choose platform
- create engagement loops
- use community for customer research, advocacy, launches, and retention
- define moderation rules and metrics

## Inputs

- product and audience
- current community assets
- platform preference
- business goal
- available capacity
- moderation constraints

## Output

- community positioning
- platform recommendation
- launch plan
- weekly engagement plan
- moderation rules
- metrics

## Related skills

product-marketing, customer-research, social, launch, referrals
