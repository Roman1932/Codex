# cold-email

Use when writing B2B cold emails and follow-up sequences that get replies.

## Main jobs

- write first-touch outreach emails
- create follow-up sequences
- personalize by segment
- improve subject lines and CTAs
- adapt tone for founders, agencies, partners, buyers, or creators

## Inputs

- offer
- target prospect
- reason for outreach
- desired CTA
- prospect data
- tone and language
- proof points

## Output

- subject lines
- first email
- follow-up sequence
- personalization variables
- CTA options
- deliverability notes

## Related skills

product-marketing, prospecting, sales-enablement, revops
