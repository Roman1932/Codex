# pricing

Use when working on pricing decisions, packaging, monetization strategy, tiers, discounts, trials, or plan presentation.

## Main jobs

- evaluate pricing model
- improve packaging
- define tiers
- compare price-value perception
- plan discounts or trials
- reduce pricing-page friction
- create pricing tests

## Inputs

- product and buyer
- current pricing
- competitors
- cost/margin if relevant
- conversion data
- customer objections
- business goal

## Output

- pricing diagnosis
- packaging recommendations
- tier structure
- price presentation notes
- test ideas
- risk notes

offers, cro, paywalls, product-marketing, customer-research
