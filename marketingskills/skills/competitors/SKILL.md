# competitors

Use when creating competitor comparison or alternative pages for SEO and sales enablement.

## Main jobs

- create comparison page structure
- write fair pros/cons
- position your product against competitors
- handle objections
- produce alternative-page copy
- support sales battlecards and SEO content

## Inputs

- your product context
- competitor names and URLs
- target audience
- buying criteria
- comparison angle
- proof points

## Output

- page outline
- comparison framework
- differentiators
- fair competitor notes
- CTA guidance
- SEO sections
- sales-use notes

## Related skills

product-marketing, competitor-profiling, copywriting, seo-audit, sales-enablement
