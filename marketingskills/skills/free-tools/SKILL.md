# free-tools

Use when planning, evaluating, or building a free tool for marketing: lead generation, SEO acquisition, virality, or product-led growth.

## Main jobs

- generate free tool ideas
- evaluate fit with product and audience
- define tool workflow and output
- connect tool to lead capture
- plan SEO and distribution
- prioritize by impact and feasibility

## Inputs

- product and audience
- customer pain points
- desired acquisition channel
- technical capacity
- examples of similar tools
- conversion goal

## Output

- free tool concepts
- prioritization
- tool specification
- landing page outline
- lead capture strategy
- distribution plan

product-marketing, content-strategy, seo-audit, lead-magnets, offers
