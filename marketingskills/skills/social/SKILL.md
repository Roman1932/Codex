# social

Use when creating, scheduling, or optimizing social media content for LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest, and other social platforms.

## Main jobs

- create social posts
- adapt content to platform
- plan content calendar
- generate hooks and captions
- define content pillars
- repurpose long-form content
- improve engagement and conversion

## Inputs

- brand/product context
- platform
- audience
- content goal
- tone/language
- source material
- CTA/link

## Output

- post copy
- hooks
- captions
- hashtag guidance
- content calendar
- repurposing ideas
- CTA options

## Related skills

product-marketing, copywriting, video, image, launch
