# ads

Use for paid advertising strategy, campaign structure, targeting, bidding, budget allocation, optimization, and reporting across Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, TikTok, and other platforms.

## Main jobs

- choose the right ad platform
- structure campaigns and ad groups
- define budgets and scaling rules
- plan targeting and exclusions
- define bid strategy
- review CPA, ROAS, CTR, CPC, CPM
- plan retargeting and reporting

## Inputs

- product and offer
- landing page URL
- target audience
- monthly budget
- target CPA or ROAS
- current tracking state
- previous campaign results

## Output

- campaign structure
- platform recommendation
- targeting plan
- budget split
- optimization plan
- tracking checklist
- weekly review template

## Related skills

ad-creative, analytics, ab-testing, cro, copywriting
