# lead-magnets

Use when creating, planning, or optimizing lead magnets for email capture or lead generation.

## Main jobs

- choose lead magnet type
- match lead magnet to audience intent
- define value and promise
- create outline or full content
- plan opt-in page and follow-up flow
- connect to sales or nurture sequence

## Inputs

- audience
- product/offer
- traffic source
- funnel stage
- desired lead quality
- format preference

## Output

- lead magnet concept
- title options
- outline
- landing page structure
- email follow-up plan
- CTA and distribution notes

product-marketing, offers, emails, copywriting, free-tools
